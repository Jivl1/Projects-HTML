What We Explore

From near-Earth operations to interstellar probes, NovaSphere covers the full spectrum of space exploration.

🛰️ Orbital Operations

Satellite deployment, station maintenance, and Earth-observation campaigns.

  • LEO & GEO mission profiles
  • Real-time telemetry dashboards
  • Debris avoidance algorithms
  • Solar panel output monitoring

🌕 Lunar Expeditions

Crewed and uncrewed missions targeting the lunar surface and cislunar space.

  • Landing site analysis tools
  • Resource mapping overlays
  • Crew life-support modeling
  • Communication relay planning

🔴 Deep Space Probes

Interplanetary trajectories to Mars, the asteroid belt, and beyond.

  • Hohmann transfer calculators
  • Gravity-assist route planner
  • Deep Space Network scheduling
  • Long-duration power budgeting
